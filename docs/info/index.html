Info

We are at the corner of Normington and Glenmont Dr. There is some parking across the street, but only a few cars will be able to fit.

The show runs from 5:30 to 10:00 pm nightly through January 9th.

There are seven songs in the show which run on a constant loop. The full loop is about 25 minutes long. The songs are:

  • David Foster - Carol of the Bells (video)
  • Leo Moracchioli - All I Want For Christmas is You (metal cover) (video)
  • Sia - Candy Cane Lane (video)
  • Neoclassic - Christmas Dubstep (aka Dance of the Sugarplum Fairy) (video)
  • Selena Gomez - Winter Wonderland (video)
  • tobyMac - Little Drummer Boy (video)
  • John Williams - Harry Potter Medley (video)

Note that we may swap in or out songs as the season progresses, but the full loop will not go beyond 30 minutes.

To listen, tune your FM radio to 89.7. This must be an actual FM radio with tuner; FM radio apps will not work.
