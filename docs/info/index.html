Info

We are at 448 Aspinwall Drive in Benicia California. There is some parking across the street, but only a few cars will be able to fit.

2022 Schedule

The show runs from 5:00 to 9:30 pm nightly through January 9th.

There are seven songs in the show which run on a constant loop. The full loop is about 25 minutes long. The songs are:

  • Tommee Profit - O Come O Come Emmanuel
  • Ariana Grande - Santa Tell Me
  • Laurie Berkner - Jingle Bells
  • Pentatonix - Hark the Herald Angels Sing
  • David Foster - Carol of the Bells
  • Selena Gomez - Winter Wonderland
  • John Williams - Harry Potter Medley

Note that we may swap in or out songs as the season progresses, but the full loop will not go beyond 30 minutes.

To listen, tune your FM radio to 89.7. This must be an actual FM radio with tuner; FM radio apps will not work.
